At Kreative Karma Dance Academy, we are passionate about dance and dedicated to providing high-quality dance education to students of all ages and abilities.

Whether you are a beginner looking to try dance for the first time, or an experienced dancer looking to take your skills to the next level, we have a program that is right for you.

Our experienced and qualified instructors offer a wide range of dance styles, including hip-hop, contemporary and bollywood.

We invite you to browse our class offerings and schedule a visit to see our beautiful studios and meet our instructors.


We also offer specialized programs for competitive dancers and those interested in pursuing a career in dance. Our classes are designed to give you a headstart in the film Indian film industry
